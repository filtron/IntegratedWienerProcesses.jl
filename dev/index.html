IntegratedWienerProcesses.ReverseTaylor — Type
ReverseTaylor

Type for representing state-space realizations in terms of Taylor coefficients in reverse order. That is the state-vector is of the form:

x = [D^n y, D^(n-1)y, ..., Dy, y]
